Your curent financial status, don't run out of money. Average quality is VERY important, it dictates the trust the people have in you
Control how many buttons you manufacture, and how many you sell each day. You can move this slider to the maximum level you can possible make, but I wouldn't advise it, because that floods the market and makes the price crash. Also here is quality - higher quality costs more, but increases trust, allowing you to do more things. Try to balance it.
Control the buying and selling of surplus stock. You start with a moderate stock of 25 of each button. Have a lot of capital? Try buying up some buttons to drive up the price
Enact various decisions that will affect the button market in different ways. Try to become president and ensure your stranglehold on the market!

Possible Strategies

There are likely MANY more, here are just a few

The Profiteer

This is a really, really simple one. Just continually modify your manufaturing sliders to ensure you're always making $1 than you're spending. This will make progress slow, but sure.

The Investor

This is a second-stage plan to the Profiteer - once you have a few $k, buy up close to all of one market, ensuring demand is adequately high. Then wait for the price to rise. SELL SELL SELL and enjoy your money.
